Biography

Harvey Dale (Lead Singer)

Harvey Dale grew up with a passion for music at a young age. Growing up in Austin, the city of music, he found his voice. As the lead singer he hopes to make a big impact in the music scene.

Carly Rose (Lead Guitarist)

Carly Rose grew up in Los Angeles, where she learned to play guitar from her father. Inspired by her father's band, Rose decided to join one of her own. She loves coming up with solos that steal the show.

Trey Farvor (Drummer)

San Antonio's own Trey Farvor sets the beat for the band. Farvor loves listening to music and coming up with new beats on the drum.
