Biography

Harvey Dale - Lead Singer

Harvey Dale grew up with a passion for music at a young age. Growing up in Austin, the city of live music, he found his voice as the lead singer.

Carly Rose - Lead Guitarist

Carly Rose grew up in Washington D.C., where she learned to play guitar from her father. Everyday she would practice and eventually became a professional.

Trey Farvor - Drummer

San Antonio's Trey Farvor sets the beat for the band. Farvor enjoys playing the drums and creating new rhythms for the band.
